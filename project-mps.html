Context & Strategic Importance

I owned the end-to-end strategic sourcing and supplier governance for the Managed Print Services (MPS) category across Ola’s entire footprint—including Corporate HQ, the Futurefactory, the Gigafactory, and regional outlets.

MPS was a high-volume OPEX category with critical cost and sustainability implications. My mandate was to reduce Total Cost of Ownership (TCO), embed responsible sourcing principles, and ensure uninterrupted operations during a period of rapid scale-up.

Scope of Ownership

Hardware & Service

  • Enterprise MFDs (Printers, Copiers, Scanners)
  • Cost-per-page (CPP) contracts
  • Consumables & Spare Parts Lifecycle
  • Preventive & Breakdown Maintenance

Governance & Commercials

  • SLA Definition, Enforcement & Penalties
  • Monthly Billing Governance
  • Device Deployment & Decommissioning
  • Consumption Control & Waste Reduction

Strategic Sourcing Strategy

1

Data-Led Diagnosis

Identified legacy inefficiencies: unbenchmarked contracts, oversized/underutilized devices, and weak SLAs. Confirmed MPS as a strategic sourcing opportunity rather than a tactical renegotiation.

2

Structured RFx & Proof of Concept

Executed a pan-India RFx followed by a rigorous Proof of Concept (PoC) phase to de-risk supplier selection.

PoC Criteria: Real-world CPP validation, device duty-cycle reliability, SLA response times, and IT security compatibility.
3

Tier-1 OEM Ecosystem

Evaluated and managed global OEMs including Xerox, Ricoh, HP, Canon, and Brother, engaging them either directly or via certified partners based on site criticality.

Responsible Sourcing & ESG

Sustainability was a core decision criterion, not an afterthought. I implemented initiatives to drive behavioral change and align MPS with Ola's corporate responsibility goals.

Consumption Governance

Role-based access controls and employee-level print monitoring via governance software.

Waste Reduction

Secure pull-printing to eliminate abandoned jobs; Default duplex & grayscale settings.

Circular Economy

Mandated OEM cartridge and hardware take-back programs.

Energy Efficiency

Transitioned fleet to Energy Star–certified devices.

Cross-Functional Leadership

Acted as the single point of ownership, coordinating between:

  • IT Team Network security & software compatibility
  • Facilities Site readiness & device placement
  • Finance Budgeting, savings validation & invoice control
  • Legal MSA, SLA penalties, and exit clauses
