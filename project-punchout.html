The Challenge

Procurement of swag and promotional items across global teams was inefficient and fragmented. Despite using the same suppliers worldwide, purchases were executed via repeated, ad-hoc POs. This resulted in no fixed global pricing, excessive manual effort, fragmented logistics, and a higher carbon footprintâ€”diverting critical bandwidth away from strategic sourcing.

Strategic Sourcing Strategy

1

Demand Consolidation & RFQ

Consolidated global demand and conducted a structured RFQ across regional and global suppliers (including Brandvia). Negotiated fixed global rate cards and slab-based pricing for high-volume items.

2

Process Automation (Coupa Integration)

Implemented supplier-hosted Punch-out catalogs in Coupa with pre-approved SKUs. This enabled business users to order directly (self-service) while automating PR-to-PO conversion and enforcing budget controls.

3

Logistics Optimization

Negotiated direct-to-recipient shipping from supplier warehouses, eliminating internal re-handling. This significantly reduced lead times and lowered transportation-related carbon emissions.

Sustainability & Responsible Sourcing

Responsible procurement principles were embedded into the design to align with ESG goals:

Carbon Reduction

Direct-to-recipient shipping reduced unnecessary transport and re-handling logistics.

Waste Minimization

Demand governance controls reduced over-ordering and inventory obsolescence.

Paperless Process

Digital catalogs and automated approvals eliminated manual paperwork.

Consolidated Shipments

Lowered carbon intensity through volume consolidation strategies.

Operational Impact

  • Zero Pricing Leakage: Post-contract pricing locked in system.

  • Bandwidth Freed: Procurement shifted from tactical execution to strategic governance.

  • Improved Compliance: 100% visibility on global spend for promotional categories.

Buyer Value Proposition

"This initiative demonstrates my ability to combine strategic sourcing with digital procurement tools (Coupa). I transformed a fragmented, low-value category into a governed, automated, and sustainable model."
