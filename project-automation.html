Context & Change Environment

"These initiatives were executed during the early phase of a newly restructured procurement operating model. There was a clear need to support adoption through system-led enablement, rather than relying on manual coordination."

The strategic objective was to stabilize operations quickly, reduce friction, and allow teams to focus on value-adding work by embedding the new procurement structure directly into system logic.

Business Challenges Identified

Procurement Intake Issues

  • • PRs frequently mis-routed during transition.
  • • Buyers spending hours manually triaging.
  • • Increased PR-to-PO cycle time due to rework.

Manual Approval Workflows

  • • Leave requests handled via informal emails.
  • • No standardized approval trail.
  • • Lack of transparency and auditability.
Solution 1

PR Assignment Automation (Coupa + VBA)

Technical Architecture

1

Coupa Report Extraction

Automated custom report extracting new PRs, including Category, Supplier, Spend Type, and Geography.

2

Excel VBA Logic Engine

Macro triggered every 3 hours applying routing logic based on Buyer Responsibility + Spend Thresholds.

3

Automated Email Routing

PR assignment emails sent automatically to the correct buyer with deep links and priority indicators.
